<p class="mb-4">A simple task management application running on the browser, built on the concept of "task management for those who don't want to try too hard."</p>

<h4 class="font-bold text-appleDark mb-3 mt-8">Application Features</h4>
<ul class="space-y-4 text-sm text-appleDark/80 bg-black/5 p-5 rounded-xl border border-black/10">
  <li class="flex items-start">
    <i class="fa-solid fa-face-smile text-appleBlue mt-0.5 mr-3 w-4 text-center shrink-0"></i>
    <div><strong class="text-appleDark block mb-1">Gentle Design</strong>Incomplete tasks are automatically carried over to the next day. It's okay if you have days where you can't complete your tasks.</div>
  </li>
  <li class="flex items-start">
    <i class="fa-solid fa-clock-rotate-left text-appleBlue mt-0.5 mr-3 w-4 text-center shrink-0"></i>
    <div><strong class="text-appleDark block mb-1">Don't Dwell on the Past</strong>Completed tasks older than a week are automatically deleted. This design allows you to always focus on the "now."</div>
  </li>
  <li class="flex items-start">
    <i class="fa-solid fa-stopwatch text-appleBlue mt-0.5 mr-3 w-4 text-center shrink-0"></i>
    <div><strong class="text-appleDark block mb-1">Rough Time Management</strong>Instead of minute-by-minute scheduling, the day is divided into "3-hour blocks". You can manage tasks intuitively just by dropping them into these blocks.</div>
  </li>
  <li class="flex items-start">
    <i class="fa-solid fa-mobile-screen text-appleBlue mt-0.5 mr-3 w-4 text-center shrink-0"></i>
    <div><strong class="text-appleDark block mb-1">Installable as an App (PWA Supported)</strong>You can install it on your device like a native app from your smartphone or PC browser. Using a Service Worker, it works even offline once you've accessed it.</div>
  </li>
</ul>
