<p class="mb-4">Every year, large quantities of unused or barely-used textbooks were being discarded at school. At the same time, it was clear that some students genuinely couldn't afford learning materials.</p>

<p class="mb-4">To bridge this gap, we started STARCLE with two high school classmates — a name I coined from "STudy Aid ReCycLE."</p>

<h4 class="font-bold text-appleDark mb-3 mt-8">How It Worked</h4>

<p class="mb-4">We collected donated textbooks, stored them in an empty dormitory room, and shipped them nationwide. Initially, recipients paid only the shipping fee (via letter pack). Later, prize money from volunteer competitions allowed us to cover shipping costs as well, making the service entirely free end-to-end. We received orders from across Japan and distributed dozens to over 100 textbooks in total.</p>
