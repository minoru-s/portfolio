<p class="mb-4">During my second year of high school, I entered two business competitions. Neither resulted in an award, but both were meaningful experiences in structuring ideas into business proposals and presenting them under evaluation.</p>

<h4 class="font-bold text-appleDark mb-3 mt-8">Career Koshien (キャリア甲子園)</h4>

<p class="mb-4">A national business idea competition for high school students.</p>

      Dynamic pricing — where prices fluctuate based on supply and demand — is well-established in aviation and hotels, but limited elsewhere. I proposed a framework to expand its application across broader industries and everyday contexts.
